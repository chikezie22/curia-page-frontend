KNOW YOUR CURIA OFFICERS

Our Lady Virgin Most Prudent Curia, Fatima Jos currently have 4 officer. Bro Emmanuel Duru the curia president,was appointed in the year 2018. He is also a corresponding secretary and a vice president, of Our Lady of Perpetual Help.
Bro Francis Anene joined the Legion of Mary in the year 2007. He is the current Secretary of Virgin Most Amiable presidium. He was elected as the Curia Vice President on December, 2020 and is serving in that capacity till date haven been re-elected for a second term.
Brother Cornelius Ogochukwu Okonkwo, joined the senior Praesidium of the Legion of Mary in the year 2005, and is currently the secretary of our Lady Virgin Most Prudent Curia Fatima and was elected on the 28th of November 2021. He is also the president of our Lady Most Admirable Praesidium appointed on the 23rd of May 2021. He has previously served as the vice-president of the same Praesidium for two terms. To the glory of God, He is also the Chairperson of The Devotion Committee of The Legion of Mary in Fatima. Sis Francisca Nwokocha who is the current Curia Treasurer also serving her first tenure to the glory of God.
The curia also appointed Bro Vitus as the Chairperson of the marriage commitee, and also appointed Bro Amadi Franklin and his assistant, Bro Chikezie Simon as the moderators and handlers of the patrician meetings in the parish.
