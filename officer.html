KNOW YOUR CURIA OFFICERS

Our Lady Virgin Most Prudent Curia, Fatima Jos, is currently led by a team of four officers. Brother Emmanuel Duru serves as the Curia President, having been appointed in 2018. He also holds multiple leadership positions within the organization: Head Corresponding Secretary for Jos Regia, Corresponding Secretary for the Bauchi Committee, Regia Assistant Secretary, and Vice President of Our Lady of Perpetual Help.
Brother Francis Anene's Legion of Mary journey began in 2007. Currently, he serves as the Secretary of the Virgin Most Amiable presidium. Additionally, he was elected Curia Vice President in December 2020 and continues to serve in this capacity, having been re-elected for a second term.
Brother Cornelius Ogochukwu Okonkwo, a dedicated member of the Legion of Mary since 2005, currently wears multiple hats within the organization. He serves as the secretary of Our Lady Virgin Most Prudent Curia Fatima, having been elected to this position on November 28th, 2021. Additionally, he demonstrates his leadership by holding the presidency of Our Lady Most Admirable Praesidium, appointed on May 23rd, 2021. Notably, his commitment extends further, as he previously served as the vice-president of the same Praesidium for two terms. To the glory of God, Brother Cornelius also contributes as the Chairperson of the Devotion Committee of the Legion of Mary in Fatima.
Sister Francisca Nwokocha also contributes significantly, currently serving her first term as the Curia Treasurer.
The Curia further bolstered its leadership by appointing Brother Vitus as the Chairperson of the Marriage Committee. Additionally, they entrusted Brothers Amadi Franklin and Chikezie Simon, a formidable team, to moderate and handle the patrician meetings within the parish.
